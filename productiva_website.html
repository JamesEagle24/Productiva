Daniel Reece

Our Founder, Talent Partner & Headhunter

Like most I fell into recruitment. Building teams from the ground up, helping clients to make game changing senior hires, it gives me a buzz and I work very hard to deliver.

I have spent the last few years recruiting Tech and Product professionals with some of the world's largest brands. Alongside recruitment however, two of my favourite hobbies are Product and Web Design.

In lockdown I spent almost all of my time immersing myself into Product and Web Design – endless hours on Udemy! Having become so fascinated by Product and Web Design I could no longer sit on the sidelines and had to do something with my passion.

Whilst I would love to be a Product Designer or full time Web Designer, I think my efforts are best suited focused on what I do best – recruitment. Productiva was born.
